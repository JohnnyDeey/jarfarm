About JAR Farm

Built to lift Rwandan farmers into global markets

JAR is an export-focused agribusiness sourcing fresh chilies, French beans, avocados, tomatoes, pineapples and more from our own farms and a trusted outgrower network. Quality, traceability, and fair pay are at the heart of everything we do.

From seed to shelf, we manage the full journey — GAP training for farmers, packhouse processing, cold chain logistics, and export certification.
