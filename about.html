Our Story

About JAR Farm

An export-focused agribusiness built from Rwanda's soil, with a vision that reaches global markets.

Our Mission

Lifting Rwandan farmers into global markets

JAR Farm was founded on a simple belief — that Rwanda's farmers grow some of the finest produce in Africa, and they deserve access to the markets that will pay a fair price for it.

We built JAR to bridge that gap. From the hillside farms of rural Rwanda to the supermarket shelves of Europe and the Middle East, we manage the full journey with care, traceability and respect for every farmer in our network.

The name JAR stands for the farmers, the land, and the vision. Every jar, every crate, every shipment carries that with it.

Our Journey

How JAR Farm came to be

Business Registration & Legal Foundation

JAR Farm officially registered in Rwanda. All legal, documentation and compliance frameworks put in place — the full foundation for a legitimate agribusiness operation.

First Produce Sales — Local Markets

Initial stock purchased and sold to stores and supermarkets in Kigali. First revenues generated, proving the market demand and building operational confidence.

Operations Manager On The Ground

Dedicated operations manager appointed in Kigali with full autonomy to grow the business. Outgrower partnerships being built and farm land being secured.

🚀 June 2025 — Full Farming Launch

Large-scale farming operations begin. 10+ acres under cultivation, 50–100 outgrowers contracted, packhouse partnerships activated, and first export lots targeted.

What We Offer

A complete agri-export operation

From seed to shelf — we handle every step so buyers get quality and farmers get paid fairly.

🌾

Own Farm Operations

10+ acres of directly managed farmland producing premium export crops including chili, French beans and avocados.

🤝

Outgrower Network

50–100 contracted outgrower farmers receiving GAP training, quality seeds on credit and guaranteed offtake at fair prices.

Packhouse & Cold Chain

Produce sorted, graded and packed at our Gikondo packhouse partner facility, with refrigerated logistics to airport.

Export Certification

Full NAEB export facilitation, phytosanitary documentation and GlobalG.A.P. compliance pathway for EU market access.

🔍

Traceability

Batch coding and audit-ready records from farm to destination. Full transparency for buyers and compliance with import regulations.

🛒

Local Distribution

Strong retail channels in Kigali supermarkets and regional cross-border distribution for second-grade and seasonal produce.
